Our "First" Moments

I remember the first time I felt butterflies in my stomach when you called me. You were the one who initiated it, and I was so excited that I couldn’t stop smiling. Such a simple moment, yet it made me so incredibly happy.

Our first FaceTime… You looked so handsome, and I couldn’t believe it. I kept thinking, How is this amazing guy calling me rn?

Our first official meet—it wasn’t even a date, just us getting to know each other. But it felt so comfortable, so natural, like I had already been on countless dates with you before.

Our first time playing valorant together at Novoid! It was so much fun finally sitting side by side, playing together. I love how supportive you were and how excited we both were for that moment.

Our first badminton game together! Do you remember getting lost in KTM? HAHAHA. That was also the first time you met my sister and friends, and they instantly loved you. No surprise there.

The day you refused to let me take the bus alone and decided to ride with me instead. That moment summed up exactly what kind of boyfriend you are—so thoughtful, so caring.

Our first time eating oden at Family Mart! I went all the way to Lendu just to see you and satisfy my craving, and you made it so worth it.

Our first big fight… We ended up at Pantai Puteri, both of us in tears, but even then, we chose to work through it. And I’m so glad that, to this day, we always do.

Your first tournament! Watching you play was mesmerizing. Even though you didn’t win, you were absolutely worth cheering for. And yes, even if we both got sunburned, HAHAHA.

Our first Sukiya! It was so good, but I still remember how sweaty you were because you thought it was spicy. HAHAHA. We went with Eisya and Ammar too, which made it even more fun.

Right after sukiya, we had our first trip to Genting with friends! It was freezing cold, and I loved how playful we were. I can’t wait to go back, just the two of us, to experience all the rides and explore everything together.

Celebrating your birthday was one of the best moments ever. Even with all the obstacles, everything still felt so perfect—maybe because we were together the whole time.

Do you remember how clingy Raisha was when she first met you? (She still is though urgh) She really thought I got a boyfriend just for her! But I love how close you two are now—it makes me so happy.

Our trip to Kenanga Mall to find my Raya outfit with Eisya and Suffeiya. It was Ramadan, scorching hot, yet you endured it all just to be with me. That kind of love and effort never goes unnoticed.

Our first Raya at your house… I still feel bad for not making it on time, but I’ll always appreciate how you held an open house just so we could celebrate together. That meant so much to me.

Our first photobooth at MyTown! I love our pictures so much—we really do look like we’re made for each other. A match made in heaven.

The time you bought donuts for my family, and Mama was so touched that she even posted you on her status. That’s when I knew—you were winning in life for sure.

Our first time exchanging flowers, ones we handpicked ourselves. It was the cutest thing ever, and seeing you receive your first flower made my heart so full. You were so thoughtful in taking care of them too, which made it even more special.

Firdaus’s wedding gave you the chance to meet more of my big family. It was chaotic and exhausting, but you handled everything so well. You made sure they loved you, and trust me, they really do.

The effort you put into celebrating my birthday was beyond sweet. You planned everything so carefully, and you even came all the way to KL just for me. That kind of effort speaks louder than words.

And finally, our first anniversary… I couldn’t have asked for anything more. You gave me everything I could ever want—from thoughtful gifts to all the love in the world. Every single thing we did meant so much to me.
